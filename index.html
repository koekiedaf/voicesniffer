VoiceSniffer

Voice to text — fast, private, and entirely on your Mac.

Double-press Option (⌥⌥) anywhere to start dictating, and your words are transcribed and pasted straight into whatever app you're in. No window-switching, no copy-paste.

Three modes

  • Chat — quick everyday dictation into any text field. On-device, instant.
  • Review — long monologues and document dictation at the highest quality, with your own custom vocabulary.
  • Meeting — conversations and calls with automatic speaker labels. Fully offline, or use the optional Gladia cloud engine.

Private by design

Chat and Review run completely on-device — your audio never leaves your Mac. No account, no analytics, no tracking. See the Privacy Policy.
